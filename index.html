DATA STRUCTURES

Data structures refer to the way data is organized, stored, and manipulated in computer memory. They provide a systematic way to manage and organize data effectively to optimize operations such as searching, inserting, deleting, and modifying data. There are various types of data structures, each designed for specific purposes and use cases.
